How it works?
  1. We will be contacting you to discuss on the pricing and contractual leasing terms of your parking space. (I have attached our standard agreement template here for your perusal).
  2. Once we have agreed, we will sign the agreement and schedule a meet up with you to show us the space and collect the necessary access devices.
  3. We usually already have a suitable driver on a waiting list. We will share all the personal details of the driver to you (Name, Car Registration, Car Model, Car Colour, and Company) for your information & registration with the management if necessary.
  4. ParkIt will handle all collections and payments. Rental shall be paid monthly by the 7th day from contract start date. 
  5. If a driver provides a notice to discontinue (1 month), we will inform you and start looking for a replacement. Once a replacement is found, their personal details will be similarly shared with you and your prior approval obtained before renting out your parking. Whether or not ParkIt finds a replacement in time, this will not affect your rental payment.

Benefits
  1. You get to earn passive income from your un-utilised parking space.
  2. You get to help out a driver in need.
  3. With RM200* per parking space, that equals RM2400 per annum. Equivalent to a fixed deposit in a bank at 3% return per annum, you will need to invest RM80,000 in the bank for a year. Furthermore, investing in the parking space is much more liquid with a 1 month termination notice only.
  4. Opportunity cost. You can use that RM80,000 for other investment opportunities!
  5. Supporting the shared economy business. There is always a deadweight loss when it comes to conventional business models. Commercial developers have to build new parking spaces to cater to demand.
These spaces are located in residential buildings. Why build more parking lots and multi level parking when you can increase supply by allowing individual parking owners to rent theirs out?

*Note: These prices are based on the market rate in that area.

Security

  1. We interview the driver to verify their identity and ensure that they are renting the parking space because they need the parking space. We will forward their details ( Car registration number, full name, valid driving license and valid business card) to you for your reference.
  2. ParkIt does not allow the driver to access any other floors apart from the car park and lobby levels. Furthermore, we do not share the details of your unit number. Only the car park level and the bay number is shared with the driver.
  3. We have been contacting the building management of several residential buildings where PARKIT has a large presence. We are looking into how we can work with them to bring PARKIT to owners. Without buy-in from a good number of owners, it is unlikely approaching management will be fruitful.
  4. All payments are performed cashless and via bank transfer.
